Warranty Policy

E-Waste Management Repair offers you a 6 month + 1 month breakage warranty on every mobile screen repaired/replaced by us from the date of invoice. We also provide 3 months warranty on all the other spare parts that we replace

You can claim the warranty by writing to us at support@cashify.in with the service details and the invoice delivered to you at the time of repair. If you need any further assistance, please connect via Chat or write to us at support@E-Waste Management.in

Warranty is limited to the parts and/or service(s) that were paid for. If only parts were purchased, warranty is limited to the replacement of the parts. If parts and repair service were purchased, warranty extends to cover the labor cost of part replacement and any other repairs specifically resulting from the initial repair.
